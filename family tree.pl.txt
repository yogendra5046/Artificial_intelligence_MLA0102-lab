% ----- Rules -----
% A person X is a parent of Y if X is the mother or father of Y.
parent(X, Y) :- father(X, Y).
parent(X, Y) :- mother(X, Y).

% A person X is a grandparent of Z if X is a parent of Y and Y is a parent of Z.
grandparent(X, Z) :- parent(X, Y), parent(Y, Z).

% A person X is a grandmother if X is a female parent of Y who is parent of Z.
grandmother(X, Z) :- mother(X, Y), parent(Y, Z).

% A person X is a grandfather if X is a male parent of Y who is parent of Z.
grandfather(X, Z) :- father(X, Y), parent(Y, Z).
